## First Section

Write in E-prime (avoid forms of "to be": is, am, are, was, were, be, being, been).
Use fair witness voice — observe without interpretation.

### Conventions

- **Glossary terms**: Link to [term name](https://unratified.org/glossary#term-id) on first use
- **Treaty acronyms**: Spell out on first mention, e.g., [International Covenant on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights (ICESCR)](https://www.ohchr.org/en/instruments-mechanisms/instruments/international-covenant-economic-social-and-cultural-rights)
- **Legislation**: Link to authoritative source, e.g., [OBBBA (P.L. 119-21)](https://www.congress.gov/)
- **Blockquotes**: Use for direct quotes, marked observations, or key takeaways

> **Key takeaway.** Summarize the main point of this section in one sentence.

## Sources

Link all claims to authoritative sources inline. Prefer government (.gov), academic (.edu), and international organization (.org) sources.
