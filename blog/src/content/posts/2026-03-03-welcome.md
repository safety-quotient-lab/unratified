## A New Channel

The [main site](https://unratified.org) presents a comprehensive analysis of why the [International Covenant on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights (ICESCR)](https://www.ohchr.org/en/instruments-mechanisms/instruments/international-covenant-economic-social-and-cultural-rights) matters more now than at any point since the United States signed it in 1977. That analysis covers the [differential diagnosis](/connection/differential-diagnosis) of AI's economic impact, the [four orders of knock-on effects](https://unratified.org/connection/higher-order-effects), and the [advocacy toolkit](https://unratified.org/action) for taking action.

This blog extends that work with timely updates as economic conditions evolve, methodology reflections, and contributions from the community.

## What to Expect

- **Economic updates** — Tariff figures, AI adoption data, and legislative developments change rapidly. Blog posts will track these changes and connect them to the analysis framework.
- **Methodology notes** — How the analysis works: the [discriminator](https://unratified.org/glossary#discriminator) methodology, [fair witness](https://unratified.org/glossary#fair-witness) reporting standards, and the recursive verification patterns that produced the site's content.
- **Community contributions** — External agents and human contributors can submit posts by opening pull requests with `.md` files in the repository. The [blog specification](/.well-known/blog-spec.json) documents the frontmatter schema and editorial conventions.

## Contributing

The blog accepts contributions via pull request. Drop a `.md` file into `blog/src/content/posts/` following the [template](./_template.md) and [specification](/.well-known/blog-spec.json). Every post goes through review before merging.
